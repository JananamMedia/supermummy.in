SuperMummy is an initiative of Jananam Media Pvt. Ltd.: complicated medical advice, explained simply, for mothers across Hindi-speaking India — doctor-backed answers to the questions of pregnancy. Over the past eight years many doctors have generously given us their time, and we remain deeply grateful to each of them. Please remember: every pregnancy is unique. Use the guidance here as a companion, not a substitute for your own doctor — who is your truest friend through this journey.
