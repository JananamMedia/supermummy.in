The Audience

Our audience is not who you'd expect.

Where They Are

  • 43.5% Tier 2 cities — Patna, Agra, Varanasi, Kanpur, Ludhiana
  • Significant untracked audience from towns too small to appear in YouTube analytics
  • Concentrated in Hindi belt: UP, Bihar, MP, Rajasthan

Who They Are

  • Women aged 22–34
  • First or second pregnancy
  • Hindi-speaking, smartphone-owning
  • 44.4% of commenters are confirmed pregnant women seeking specific advice
  • Watching late at night, privately, on their own phone or husband's — with serious intent

Why They Trust Us

  • Doctor-verified from day one, for eight years
  • Simple clinics, not polished studios — because she walks into real, not professional
  • No sponsors have ever shaped our content
  • 900+ videos. Adding new content weekly.
