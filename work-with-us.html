Partnerships

We do not take advertising.
We take partnerships.

Editorial independence is not a negotiating position. It is the reason 149,628 subscribers trust us with the most anxious moments of their pregnancies. The moment a sponsor shapes our content, the trust dissolves. And the trust is the product.

Every video carries a medical disclaimer — "Each pregnancy is unique. Recommendations and guidelines in this video are generic and you are advised to consult your doctor for your individual care & health." — in English and Hindi, on every video, for eight years without exception.

Lives depend on it.

What we offer: reach that is search-verified, intent-driven, and concentrated in exactly the geographies and demographics that Hindi maternal health brands need to reach.

What we require: that nothing goes in front of our audience that a qualified doctor has not cleared.

The right partners
  • Maternal nutrition & prenatal supplements
  • Diagnostic centres & pathology chains
  • Pregnancy-safe personal care
  • Maternal health insurance
  • Telehealth platforms serving Hindi-speaking women
  • Baby care in the first year
The wrong partners
  • Weight loss products
  • Fast food
  • Any health claim we cannot verify medically
  • Anything we would not show to a woman in her first trimester
