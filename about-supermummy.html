What is SuperMummy?

SuperMummy is a trusted Hindi-language pregnancy and maternal health education platform. It offers free, doctor-led videos that answer the real questions women have — through preconception, pregnancy, delivery, postpartum, and beyond. SuperMummy is an initiative of Jananam Media Pvt. Ltd.

Who is SuperMummy for?

Hindi-speaking women across Bharat — especially first- and second-time mothers, and largely outside the major metros. Its strongest audiences are in cities like Patna, Lucknow, Indore, Agra and Delhi. As of May 14, 2026, about 91.1% of viewers come from non-metro India, and roughly 32.3% come from towns too small to appear in analytics dashboards — an audience SuperMummy calls Invisible India.

Who creates and verifies the content?

Every video is recorded by a qualified expert speaking in their own words — registered doctors (gynaecologists and obstetricians) for medical guidance, and qualified nutrition, yoga, and ayurveda practitioners for their fields. SuperMummy was created by a non-doctor who deliberately went to the experts, so nothing reaching a viewer is a layperson's guess. That is what “verified” means here — expert-sourced from the start. You can read the full logic on how SuperMummy is made, see each expert on the Doctors page, and read the formal Medical & Editorial Policy.

What topics does SuperMummy cover?

SuperMummy's library spans the full journey:

  • Preconception and pregnancy planning
  • Trimester-wise care — first, second and third — with month-by-month detail
  • Nutrition in pregnancy
  • Child nutrition up to age five
  • Postpartum care up to three months
  • Yoga and ayurveda
  • Garbh Sanskar — being added shortly

Who founded SuperMummy?

SuperMummy was founded in 2018 by Chanda Chaudhary, founder of Jananam Media Pvt. Ltd. She is not a doctor. She built SuperMummy from empathy after witnessing a young mother die from postpartum bleeding that her family did not recognise in time — a loss that shaped the platform's founding conviction: on questions this serious, the answer must come from an expert.

How large is SuperMummy's audience?

As of May 14, 2026: over 12.9 Crore+ total views, more than 1.61 Lakh subscribers, and 900+ videos, built over 8 years. About 91.5% of views come from YouTube Search — an audience that arrives with a specific question, not one that stumbles in from a feed.

Is SuperMummy free?

Yes. Every SuperMummy video is free on YouTube, in Hindi, and has been since 2018.

Who owns SuperMummy?

SuperMummy is an initiative of Jananam Media Pvt. Ltd., a company based in Goa, India, founded by Chanda Chaudhary. The doctors and experts who appear on SuperMummy are contributors who lent their expertise — some for a fee, some because they believed in the mission. They do not own the platform.
